Who We Are

Serving Society Through Islamic Values

Al-Rabb Foundation is a humanitarian and educational NGO established to serve society through Islamic values, compassion, and responsibility.

Our mission is to uplift underprivileged girls by providing them with quality religious and worldly education, while also supporting orphans, widows, and impoverished families. We believe that education, dignity, and care can transform lives and build a stronger Ummah.

Our Mission

To educate, nurture, and empower girls, and to provide essential humanitarian assistance to the poor and needy, enabling them to live with dignity and hope.

Our Vision

A society where every girl receives proper education, every orphan is cared for, and every struggling family has access to basic necessities.
