Welcome to Al-Rabb Foundation

Empowering Girls. Supporting Orphans.
Serving Humanity.

To educate, nurture, and empower girls, and to provide essential humanitarian assistance to the poor and needy, enabling them to live with dignity and hope.

  • Religious & Worldly Education
  • Orphan & Widow Support
  • Sustainable Humanity Projects

A society where every girl receives proper education, every orphan is cared for, and every struggling family has access to basic necessities and Islamic guidance.

  • Zero Literacy Gap
  • Secure Future for Orphans
  • Strong Morals & Dignity

Why Support Al-Rab?

Empowering the underprivileged through faith-based compassion and sustainable humanitarian aid.

Holistic Girls' Education

Bridging the gap between religious excellence and modern academic success for underprivileged girls.

Compassionate Orphan Care

Providing a family environment, nutrition, and education to ensure orphans live with dignity.

Sustainable Food Security

Distributing nutritious meals and ration packs to widows and vulnerable families across Pakistan.

Essential Medical Aid

Facilitating healthcare access and life-saving medicine for those who cannot afford basic treatment.

Clean Water Projects

Installing hand pumps and filtration systems to provide safe drinking water to rural communities.

Ramadan & Zakat Fulfillment

Managing your donations with Amanah to provide Iftar and Eid support to the most vulnerable.

100% Trust Policy

Built on transparency, ensuring your Sadaqah reaches those in genuine need across Pakistan.
